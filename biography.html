Biography

Short Bio

    Wei Zhang is currently an associate researcher with the title of Zijiang Young Scholar in the School of Computer Science and Technology, East China Normal University (ECNU). His research is focused on user data mining, especially for personalized representation learning and recommendation. He got his Ph.D. in the department of Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University and his B.E. in the school of Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology. He has authored more than 20 research papers in CCF-A journals and conferences. He is serving or ever served as an area chair in ICDM'19 and program committee members in the top conferences of ACM SIGKDD, ACM SIGIR, WWW, ACL, AAAI, IJCAI, etc. He got ACM SIGMOD China Rising Star Award in 2020, the 1st place of ACM Multimedia Grand Challenge for Social Media Prediction in 2020 and the 5th place of the KDD Cup 2020 Challenges for Modern E-Commerce Platform: Debiasing in 2020.
