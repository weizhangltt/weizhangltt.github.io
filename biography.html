Biography

Short Bio

    Wei Zhang is currently a professor at the School of Computer Science and Technology, East China Normal University (ECNU). His research is focused on data mining, machine learning, recommender systems and artificial intelligence for education. He got his Ph.D. in the Department of Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University and his B.E. in the School of Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology. He has authored more than 40 research papers in CCF-A journals and conferences. He is serving or ever served as program committee members in the top conferences of SIGKDD, SIGIR, WWW, ICDE, AAAI, IJCAI, ACL, MM, etc.
