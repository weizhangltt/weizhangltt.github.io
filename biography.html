Biography

Short Bio

    Wei Zhang is currently an associate researcher with the title of Zijiang young scholar in the School of Computer Science and Technology, East China Normal University (ECNU). Before that, he got his Ph.D. in the department of Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jianyong Wang from Tsinghua database group. He got his B.E. degree, with the major of information security in the department of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology. He visited the data mining research group in Computer Science, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, under the supervision of Prof. Jiawei Han. He was also a member of HIT-CIR, advised by Prof. Bing Qin.
